Scientific Skepticism

Many scientists and researchers believe that astral projection is a psychological phenomenon rather than a literal separation of consciousness from the body. Neurological studies suggest that out-of-body experiences may be linked to brain activity rather than an actual astral realm.

Common Misconceptions

  • It's dangerous: While some believe astral projection can be risky, there is no evidence of physical harm occurring from the practice.
  • Only a few people can do it: Many techniques exist, and with practice, most people can experience some form of out-of-body sensation.
  • It's always vivid: Some experiences are clear, while others feel more like lucid dreams or faint impressions.
