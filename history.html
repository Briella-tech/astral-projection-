Ancient Perspectives

The concept of astral projection has roots in many ancient cultures, including Egyptian, Greek, and Hindu traditions. These civilizations described out-of-body experiences in their texts and spiritual practices.

Modern Interpretations

In contemporary times, astral projection is often associated with the New Age movement and is practiced through meditation, hypnosis, and guided visualization.
