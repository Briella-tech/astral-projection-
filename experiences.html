First-Hand Accounts

Many individuals have shared their experiences of astral projection. Some describe a floating sensation, while others talk about visiting different realms or encountering spiritual beings.

Common Themes in Experiences

  • A sense of detachment from the physical body.
  • Encountering beings or guides in the astral plane.
  • Visiting distant places or entirely different dimensions.
  • A feeling of deep peace and understanding.
